Frequently asked questions

Do I need a Notion account?
No. ContentAI Studio runs entirely in your browser. There's nothing to install, no Notion account needed, and no duplication steps. Just sign up and start generating.
What AI model powers the content generation?
ContentAI Studio uses Claude (by Anthropic) — one of the most capable AI models available — to generate all content. It's the same model used in Claude.ai.
What counts as a "generation"?
Each time you click "Generate content" and receive output, that counts as one generation. Whether you're generating a caption, 5 hooks, or a full hashtag set — each request is one generation.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, cancel anytime directly from the billing portal inside the app. You'll keep access until the end of your paid billing period. No questions asked.
Do generations reset each month?
Yes. Your monthly generation count resets at the start of each billing cycle. Unused generations don't roll over.
